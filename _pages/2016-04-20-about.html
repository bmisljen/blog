Welcome!

I am Boris Misljencevic, a computer engineer by training who specializes in software consulting and instructing eLearning courses. I work remotely from both Toronto and Ottawa, Canada on a contract basis and have worked with clients from small startups to large multinational corporations. I offer free in person, Skype or phone consultations regarding my services.

I hold a bachelor's degree in computer engineering from Carleton University in Ottawa Canada and have work experience with founding successful product startups such as Episync as well as multiple years of software design and development experience in large silicon valley based tech companies. Over the past three years I have transitioned to working as an independent software consultant and E-learning technology instructor.

My technical experience lies with hot technologies such as: Cloud Engineering (Openstack, Azure, Amazon EC2), Big Data (Hadoop, Google Cloud Platform), RabbitMQ, SQL/PostgreSQL, Performance Engineering, Web Apps (Django, Javascript, HTML/CSS). I also specialize in various programming languages such as: Python, Java, C/C++ and JavaScript.
